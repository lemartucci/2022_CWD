Chronic Wasting Disease

Welcome! This is a tool to compare the spread of Chronic Wasting Disease in six midwestern states over time. Chronic Wasting Disease is an always fatal and contagious neurological disease that affects deer, elk, and moose. It was first observed in the 1960s and has since spread to 27 U.S. states and two Canadian provinces. This interactive map and coordinated visualization focuses on Wisconsin, Minnesota, Illinois, Iowa, Missouri and Michigan.....
