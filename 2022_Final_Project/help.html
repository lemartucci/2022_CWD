Help

Frequently Asked Questions:



    1. How do I interact with the map?

    To interact with the map, first start by selecting a year from the dropdown menu titled "Select Year" in the upper left hand corner of the website. Select a different year and watch the map update; observe how the number of positive CWD cases has changed over time. If you are interested in what factors may contribute to the uneven spread of CWD in the midwest, select an attribute from the dropdown menu titled "Select Overlay". Observe differences in numbers of deer licenses sold and deer harvested across the five states.

    2. Something isn't working. What do I do?

    If you encounter a technical problem with the website, please report your bug using this Google Form. We appreciate your feedback and will fix the issue as soon as possible!
